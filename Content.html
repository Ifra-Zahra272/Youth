Introduction

What is Youth?

Youth" is a word that refers to the time of life when someone is young, or the state of being young. It can also refer to young people in general.

Defination

The period of life from childhood to adulthood The appearance, freshness, vigor, and spirit of a young person The first or early period of anything Young persons collectively A young person, especially a young man or male adolescent

Examples

"I was a good football player in my youth" "It's quite a job, training the youth of the country to be mature, responsible citizens" "Our organization helps to shape character, citizenship, and fitness in these youths"

United Nations definition

The United Nations defines "youth" as people between the ages of 15 and 24.

INTERNATIONAL YOUTH DAY

International Youth Day, celebrated annually on August 12th, was established by the UN General Assembly in 1999, following a recommendation from the World Conference of Ministers Responsible for Youth in 1998, to raise awareness of youth issues and celebrate the potential of young people as partners in global society.
